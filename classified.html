Classified Ads

Posting an ad in the NSTP Classifieds is free of charge for NSTP Members and $40 a month for non-members. Content for the NSTP Classified Ads section can include job recruitment, and practice purchase and sales.


Member ads will automatically be posted for six-months and can be renewed for an additional six-month period. Please contact us at 800-367-8130 if you’d like to cancel your ad before the end of the six-month period.


Please note: NSTP is not liable for any promotions or claims by the postings.
